CogAT Preparation

In depth preparation for every section of the test (verbal, quantitative, and non-verbal). Before the course begins, we ask the participants to take a practice CogAT test (link shall be provided). This is optional but it is recommended to take it. Towards the end of the course, we will require the participants to take a practice test, to see their improvement.

ITBS Preparation

In depth preparation for the math and reading section. Before the course begins, we ask the participants to take a practice test (link shall be provided). This is optional but it is recommended to take it. Towards the end of the course, we will require the participants to take a practice test, to see their improvement.
