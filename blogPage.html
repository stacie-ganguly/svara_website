Svara's First Workshop

On July 18th, we held a super fun Svara Bollywood dance workshop. Everyone learned a lively routine to Lover by Diljit Dosanjh and had such a great time dancing together. We also raised $55, which we donated to OHSU. Overall, it was a fun night of dancing and giving back to our community!
