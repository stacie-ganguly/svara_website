Svara is a student-led nonprofit organization, striving to provide support and financial aid for children’s mental, emotional, and physical health. Here, we are passionate about helping children in any shape or way possible.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide essential resources and support for children in need and raise awareness on children’s overall wellbeing. This includes their mental, emotional and physical health alongside their education. By leveraging the power of dance, we strive to raise financial aid for our cause while simultaneously fostering creativity through art and discipline of dance throughout our community.

What is Svara's Story?

Svara was founded from Pooja and Teju’s shared passion for dance and helping children. With years of training in Indian Classical and Bollywood styles, they are excited to share the joy, discipline, and culture of dance with their community. Pooja’s dedication to children grew through tutoring with a nonprofit and teaching dance at her academy, where she saw the impact of encouragement and patience. These experiences led to the idea of their very own non-profit organization to make a tangible difference in the lives of children in the Portland Community. And thus, Svara was born.
