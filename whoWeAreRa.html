Our Mission

Svara's mission is to raise money for pediatric cancer research (such as OHSU) and humanitarian aid organizations (such as World Central Kitchen) through different types of fundraisers while also providing workshops and courses in our community at a low-cost. We organize Bollywood Low-Cost Workshops featuring fresh and new choreographies for all ages and experiences. Our workshops are taught by our two co-founders who are certified professional dancers trained in both Bollywood and Kathak Classical Dance. Check out our Services Page for more information!
